Sherley Sosa Lopez, M.Ed, BCBA
Sherley Sosa Lopez, BCBA since 2016, is a passionate BCBA who has worked in different settigs including clinic, school, and in home. Currently, Sherley is working as a BCBA Trainer, coaching trainees using ABA principles to conduct initial assessments, re-assessments, create programs using VB-MAPP, ABLLS, and Vineland III, and guiding caregivers in the implementation and generalization of preventive strategies and behavior management skills
